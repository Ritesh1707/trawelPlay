Detailed Day-wise Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Madurai Arrival - Transfer to Kodaikanal Upon arrival at Madurai, visit the renowned Meenakshi Temple. Afterward, begin your scenic journey towards Kodaikanal (approx. 120 km, 3-4 hours). Check into your hotel upon arrival. Known for serene landscapes and pleasant weather, Kodaikanal invites exploration. Enjoy boating on Kodaikanal Lake, stroll along Coaker's Walk for valley views, and capture photos at Pillar Rocks. Overnight stay in Kodaikanal.
  2. Day 2: Kodaikanal to Ooty – Scenic Drive After breakfast, check out and proceed to Ooty (approx. 250 km, 6-7 hours). The drive offers beautiful vistas, tea plantations, and scenic viewpoints. Check into your Ooty hotel upon arrival. Spend the evening enjoying a peaceful boat ride on Ooty Lake, followed by a relaxing visit to the Botanical Garden. Overnight stay in Ooty.
  3. Day 3: Ooty & Coonoor Sightseeing – Toy Train Ride Explore Ooty after breakfast, starting with Doddabetta Peak for panoramic views. Proceed to Coonoor (approx. 20 km), a charming nearby hill station. Experience a scenic ride on the Nilgiri Mountain Railway (Toy Train) from Coonoor to Ooty (subject to availability), enjoying views of hills and tea plantations. In Coonoor, visit Sim’s Park, Lamb’s Rock, and Dolphin’s Nose. Return to Ooty for a leisurely evening. Overnight stay in Ooty.
  4. Day 4: Ooty to Coorg – The Scotland of India After breakfast, check out and drive towards Coorg (approx. 250 km, 6-7 hours), famed for coffee plantations and lush greenery. Check into your hotel upon arrival. In the evening, visit Raja’s Seat for stunning sunset views over mist-covered hills. Explore the historical Madikeri Fort and its scenic surroundings. Overnight stay in Coorg.
  5. Day 5: Coorg Exploration – Monasteries & Waterfalls Enjoy a full day of sightseeing in Coorg after breakfast. Visit the Namdroling Monastery (Golden Temple) in Bylakuppe, a major Tibetan settlement. Head to Abbey Falls, a picturesque waterfall amidst coffee plantations. Explore Talacauvery, the source of the River Cauvery, and optionally, take a walk through coffee estates. Relax or explore local markets in the evening. Overnight stay in Coorg.
  6. Day 6: Coorg to Mangalore – Departure After breakfast, check out and journey to Mangalore (approx. 150 km, 4-5 hours). Depending on your schedule, you might visit local attractions or proceed directly to Mangalore Airport/Railway Station for your onward journey, concluding your South India exploration.

Begin sightseeing with Ooty Lake (boating optional) and the diverse Botanical Garden. Explore the Rose Garden in the evening. Overnight stay in Ooty.
  2. Day 2: Ooty & Coonoor Exploration – Toy Train Experience After breakfast, visit Doddabetta Peak for panoramic views. Drive to Coonoor (approx. 20 km).   3. Day 3: Ooty to Coorg – Scenic Transfer & Local Sights After breakfast, check out and drive to Coorg (approx. 250 km, 6-7 hours). Check into your hotel upon arrival. Explore Raja's Seat for panoramic hill views and the historical Madikeri Fort. Enjoy leisure time or explore the local market in the evening. Overnight stay in Coorg.
  4. Day 4: Coorg Sightseeing – Culture & Nature Embark on a full day of Coorg sightseeing. Visit the Namdroling Monastery (Golden Temple) in Bylakuppe. Head to the picturesque Abbey Falls. Explore Talacauvery, the origin of the River Cauvery. Time permitting, visit Iruppu Falls or stroll through coffee estates. Enjoy local shopping in the evening. Overnight stay in Coorg.
  5. Day 5: Coorg to Bengaluru – Departure After breakfast, check out and drive back to Bengaluru (approx. 270 km, 6-7 hours). You will be dropped off at Bengaluru Railway Station or Airport for your onward journey.

Detailed Day-wise Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Arrival in Bangalore – Transfer to Mysore Arrive in Bangalore and proceed to Mysore (approx. 145 km, 3.5-4 hours). Optional stops: Ranganathittu Bird Sanctuary or Tipu Sultan's Summer Palace. Check into your Mysore hotel. In the evening, visit the illuminated Mysore Palace and enjoy the Brindavan Gardens musical fountain show. Overnight stay in Mysore.
  2. Day 2: Mysore to Coorg – Heritage to Hills After breakfast, drive to Coorg (approx. 120 km, 3-3.5 hours). Check into your Coorg hotel/resort. Explore Abbey Falls, Raja's Seat (sunset views), and Madikeri Fort. Enjoy leisure time and local Coorgi cuisine. Overnight stay in Coorg.
  3. Day 3: Coorg to Ooty – Scenic Ghat Drive Start early for the drive to Ooty (approx. 230 km, 6-7 hours) via the Western Ghats. Optional break at Bandipur National Park (safari extra). Check into your Ooty hotel. Enjoy a walk by Ooty Lake in the evening (boating optional). Overnight stay in Ooty.
  4. Day 4: Ooty Local Sightseeing – Gardens & Peaks Explore Ooty after breakfast: Botanical Gardens, Doddabetta Peak (panoramic views), Tea Factory & Museum, and the Rose Garden. Optional: Nilgiri Mountain Railway ride or drive to Coonoor (Sim's Park, Dolphin's Nose). Shop for local chocolates, tea, and spices. Overnight stay in Ooty.
  5. Day 5: Ooty to Coimbatore – Departure After breakfast, check out and drive to Coimbatore (approx. 85 km, 3 hours). Optional: visit Marudamalai Temple or shop at Brookefields Mall before proceeding to Coimbatore Airport/Railway Station for departure.
