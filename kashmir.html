Detailed Day-wise Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar & Local Sightseeing Upon arrival at Srinagar Airport, you'll be greeted and transferred to your hotel. After checking in, explore the beautiful Mughal Gardens including Nishat Bagh, Shalimar Bagh, and Chashme Shahi. Visit the sacred Shankaracharya Temple and Hazratbal Shrine. Take a leisurely stroll through the old city's charming lanes and visit a local handicraft shop to witness traditional Kashmiri craftsmanship. Overnight stay at a hotel in Srinagar.
  2. Day 2: Excursion to Gulmarg (1 Night) After breakfast, drive to Gulmarg (approximately 52 km / 2 hours), known as the "Meadow of Flowers." The scenic journey offers stunning views of pine forests and picturesque landscapes. Check into your hotel and spend the day exploring this beautiful hill station. Visit the historic Maharani Temple and St. Mary's Church. Enjoy the breathtaking landscape and panoramic views. Overnight stay at a hotel in Gulmarg.
  3. Day 3: Gulmarg to Pahalgam (1 Night) After breakfast, check out and proceed to Pahalgam (approximately 140 km / 4 hours), the "Valley of Shepherds." The drive takes you through scenic villages and the breathtaking Lidder Valley. Upon arrival in Pahalgam, check into your hotel. Spend the day enjoying the serene Lidder River and exploring the local markets known for their woolens and handicrafts. Overnight stay at a hotel in Pahalgam.
  4. Day 4: Pahalgam to Srinagar (1 Night) After breakfast, drive back to Srinagar (approximately 90 km / 2.5 hours). Upon arrival, visit a local carpet-weaving factory where you can observe skilled artisans crafting exquisite Kashmiri carpets. Take a leisurely walk around Dal Lake, enjoying the scenic beauty and vibrant atmosphere. Explore Lal Chowk, the city's main market area, for shopping. Overnight stay at a hotel in Srinagar.
  5. Day 5: Srinagar to Sonmarg (1 Night) After breakfast, proceed to Sonmarg (approximately 80 km / 3 hours), known as the "Meadow of Gold." The journey takes you through beautiful Sindh Valley with its breathtaking scenery. Upon arrival, enjoy the magnificent views of Thajiwas Glacier (accessible via pony ride or local taxi at additional cost). If time and conditions permit, explore Zojila Pass and Zero Point (optional, at extra cost). Overnight stay at a hotel in Sonmarg.
  6. Day 6: Sonmarg to Srinagar (Houseboat Stay - 1 Night) After breakfast, return to Srinagar. Experience a traditional Shikara ride on the serene Dal Lake, floating past floating gardens and local life on the water. Later, check in to a traditional Kashmiri houseboat, a unique accommodation experience. Enjoy a peaceful evening watching the sunset over the lake from your houseboat deck. Overnight stay in a houseboat in Srinagar.
  7. Day 7: Departure After breakfast, check out from the houseboat. Depending on your flight time, you may have some free time for last-minute shopping or relaxation. Transfer to Srinagar Airport for your departure flight, marking the end of your memorable Kashmir journey.

Option 1: Madurai - Kodaikanal - Ooty - Coorg - Mangalore 6 Days / 5 Nights

Detailed Day-wise Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Madurai Arrival - Transfer to Kodaikanal Upon arrival at Madurai, visit the renowned Meenakshi Temple. Afterward, begin your scenic journey towards Kodaikanal (approx. 120 km, 3-4 hours). Check into your hotel upon arrival. Known for serene landscapes and pleasant weather, Kodaikanal invites exploration. Enjoy boating on Kodaikanal Lake, stroll along Coaker's Walk for valley views, and capture photos at Pillar Rocks. Overnight stay in Kodaikanal.
  2. Day 2: Kodaikanal to Ooty – Scenic Drive After breakfast, check out and proceed to Ooty (approx. 250 km, 6-7 hours). The drive offers beautiful vistas, tea plantations, and scenic viewpoints. Check into your Ooty hotel upon arrival. Spend the evening enjoying a peaceful boat ride on Ooty Lake, followed by a relaxing visit to the Botanical Garden. Overnight stay in Ooty.
  3. Day 3: Ooty & Coonoor Sightseeing – Toy Train Ride Explore Ooty after breakfast, starting with Doddabetta Peak for panoramic views. Proceed to Coonoor (approx. 20 km), a charming nearby hill station. Experience a scenic ride on the Nilgiri Mountain Railway (Toy Train) from Coonoor to Ooty (subject to availability), enjoying views of hills and tea plantations. In Coonoor, visit Sim's Park, Lamb's Rock, and Dolphin's Nose. Return to Ooty for a leisurely evening. Overnight stay in Ooty.
  4. Day 4: Ooty to Coorg – The Scotland of India After breakfast, check out and drive towards Coorg (approx. 250 km, 6-7 hours), famed for coffee plantations and lush greenery. Check into your hotel upon arrival. In the evening, visit Raja's Seat for stunning sunset views over mist-covered hills. Explore the historical Madikeri Fort and its scenic surroundings. Overnight stay in Coorg.
  5. Day 5: Coorg Exploration – Monasteries & Waterfalls Enjoy a full day of sightseeing in Coorg after breakfast. Visit the Namdroling Monastery (Golden Temple) in Bylakuppe, a major Tibetan settlement. Head to Abbey Falls, a picturesque waterfall amidst coffee plantations. Explore Talacauvery, the source of the River Cauvery, and optionally, take a walk through coffee estates. Relax or explore local markets in the evening. Overnight stay in Coorg.
  6. Day 6: Coorg to Mangalore – Departure After breakfast, check out and journey to Mangalore (approx. 150 km, 4-5 hours). Depending on your schedule, you might visit local attractions or proceed directly to Mangalore Airport/Railway Station for your onward journey, concluding your South India exploration.
