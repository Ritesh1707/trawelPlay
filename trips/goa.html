Detailed Day-wise Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Arrival & Leisure Upon arrival at Goa Airport (Dabolim/Mopa), you'll be greeted by our representative and transferred to your hotel for check-in. Spend the evening at leisure exploring nearby beaches, shacks, or local markets. Optional activities include watching the sunset at Dona Paula Viewpoint or enjoying an evening cruise on the Mandovi River (at additional cost). Relax and prepare for your exciting Goa adventure. Overnight stay in Goa.
  2. Day 2: North Goa Sightseeing After breakfast, embark on a comprehensive exploration of North Goa's stunning beaches and historical forts. Visit Fort Aguada and Taj Fort Aguada, followed by the recently opened Aguada Central Jail Museum. Enjoy the pristine waters at Sinquerim Beach and Candolim Beach. Continue to the popular Calangute Beach, Baga Beach, and Anjuna Beach. Capture memorable photos at Chapora Fort (famous as the "Dil Chahta Hai Point") and relax at Vagator Beach. End your tour with a visit to the historical Reis Magos Fort. Optional activities include dolphin spotting trips and Snow Park Adventure (at additional cost). Return to your hotel for overnight stay in North Goa.
  3. Day 3: South Goa Sightseeing After breakfast, discover the rich heritage and scenic attractions of South Goa. Visit the UNESCO World Heritage churches of Old Goa, including the Basilica of Bom Jesus (housing the remains of St. Francis Xavier) and the magnificent Se Cathedral. Explore the ruins of St. Augustine Tower and enjoy an interactive experience at the World Wax Museum. Admire the Portuguese-influenced architecture in Panjim's churches and colorful Portuguese colony. Visit the sacred Mangeshi Temple and Balaji Temple to experience Goa's religious diversity. Unwind at Dona Paula and Miramar Beach, followed by shopping at Panjim's vibrant markets for local handicrafts and souvenirs. Return to your hotel for a relaxing evening and overnight stay in South Goa.
  4. Day 4: Dudhsagar Waterfall & Spice Plantation Tour Start your day early with a journey to the magnificent Dudhsagar Waterfalls, one of India's tallest waterfalls cascading from a height of 310 meters. Marvel at this natural wonder where the white water appears like milk flowing down the Western Ghats (hence the name "Dudhsagar" or "Sea of Milk"). After enjoying the breathtaking views and taking photos, proceed to a traditional spice plantation for an educational guided tour. Learn about various spices grown in Goa and their culinary and medicinal uses. Enjoy an authentic Goan lunch at the plantation. In the evening, visit either Colva Beach or Palolem Beach to witness a stunning sunset over the Arabian Sea. Spend some free time shopping and exploring local cafés before returning to your hotel for overnight stay in Goa.
  5. Day 5: Departure from Goa After breakfast, check out from your hotel and transfer to Mopa Airport for your departure flight, taking with you wonderful memories of your Goa vacation.
