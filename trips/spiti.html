Detailed Day-wise Itinerary

  1. Day 1: Chandigarh to Narkanda Begin your journey from Chandigarh in the morning, embarking on a scenic drive (approx. 174 km, 4 hours) through the beautiful Shivalik hills and pine forests. Check into your hotel upon arrival in Narkanda. Explore Hatu Peak, the highest point in the area offering panoramic views of the surrounding Himalayan ranges. Visit the local market and nearby apple orchards. Enjoy an overnight stay in Narkanda.
  2. Day 2: Narkanda to Chitkul – The Last Village After an early breakfast, check out and drive to Chitkul (approx. 185 km, 6 hours), the last inhabited village near the Indo-Tibet border. Journey through scenic towns like Rampur and Sangla. Visit the sacred Mathi Devi Temple, enjoy a peaceful walk along the Baspa River, and explore the traditional wooden houses and lush meadows. Experience the tranquil beauty of this remote village with an overnight stay in Chitkul.
  3. Day 3: Chitkul to Kaza – Heart of Spiti Valley Embark on an early morning journey to Kaza (approx. 250 km, 8-9 hours), the administrative center of Spiti Valley. This long but rewarding drive takes you through changing landscapes, high mountain passes, and remote villages. En route, visit Nako Lake, a hidden gem nestled at 11,900 ft, and Gue Monastery, famous for its naturally preserved 500-year-old mummy. Arrive in Kaza by evening for an overnight stay.
  4. Day 4: Kaza Local Sightseeing – Monasteries & Villages Dedicate the full day to exploring Spiti Valley's cultural and natural wonders. Visit Key Monastery, the largest and oldest monastery in Spiti perched atop a hill. Continue to Kibber Village, one of the highest inhabited villages in the world. Stop at Hikkim to see the world's highest post office, then Langza with its iconic Buddha statue and fossil site. Complete your tour at Komik Village, Asia's highest village with road access. Return to Kaza for an overnight stay.
  5. Day 5: Kaza to Kalpa – Kinnaur Valley Views After breakfast, check out and journey to Kalpa (approx. 210 km, 6 hours), driving along the beautiful Spiti and Sutlej River valleys. Arrive in Kalpa by afternoon and check into your hotel. Visit Kalpa Monastery and the ancient Narayan-Nagini Temple. Marvel at the breathtaking views of the majestic Kinnaur Kailash mountain range as the evening light changes its colors. Enjoy an overnight stay in Kalpa.
  6. Day 6: Kalpa to Chandigarh – Departure After an early breakfast, check out from your hotel and begin your return journey to Chandigarh (approx. 338 km, 8 hours). The scenic drive offers one last opportunity to capture the magnificent Himalayan landscapes. Your Spiti Valley adventure concludes with arrival in Chandigarh by evening, where you'll be dropped off for your onward journey.
