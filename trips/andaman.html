Itinerary Option 1

  1. Day 1: Arrival in Port Blair Arrive at Port Blair Airport. Visit Cellular Jail (historic colonial prison) and Corbyn's Cove Beach (serene coastline). In the evening, experience the mesmerizing Light & Sound Show at Cellular Jail. Overnight stay in Port Blair.
  2. Day 2: Transfer to Havelock Island Morning cruise to Havelock Island. Visit Radhanagar Beach (voted among Asia's best beaches) with its white sands and turquoise waters. Relax and enjoy the natural beauty. Overnight stay in Havelock.
  3. Day 3: Elephant Beach & Return to Port Blair Morning speedboat ride to Elephant Beach for snorkeling (complimentary) and water activities. Visit Kalapather Beach in the afternoon. Evening cruise back to Port Blair. Overnight stay in Port Blair.
  4. Day 4: Departure After breakfast, transfer to Port Blair Airport for your onward journey with unforgettable memories of Andaman Islands.

Itinerary Option 2

  1. Day 1: Arrival & Transfer to Havelock Arrive at Port Blair Airport and immediately take a cruise to Havelock Island. Visit Radhanagar Beach (voted among Asia's best beaches). Relax and enjoy the pristine environment. Overnight stay in Havelock.
  2. Day 2: Elephant Beach Exploration Full day to enjoy Elephant Beach with speedboat ride, snorkeling (complimentary), and water activities. Visit Kalapather Beach in the evening. Overnight stay in Havelock.
  3. Day 3: Return to Port Blair & Sightseeing Morning cruise back to Port Blair. Visit Cellular Jail (historic colonial prison) and Corbyn's Cove Beach. Evening Light & Sound Show at Cellular Jail. Overnight stay in Port Blair.
  4. Day 4: Departure After breakfast, transfer to Port Blair Airport for your onward journey with beautiful memories of Andaman Islands.
