Refund Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2023

At TrawelPlay Hospitality, we strive to provide transparent and fair refund policies for our B2B partners. Please review the following terms carefully.

Cancellation & Refund Terms

All cancellation requests must be made in writing via email to accounts@trawelplayhospitality.com. Refunds will be processed according to the following schedule:

Hotel Bookings

  • Hotel cancellation policies vary by property and season
  • Some hotels may impose 100% cancellation fees during peak seasons
  • We will communicate any non-refundable hotel bookings in advance
  • Early departures are non-refundable

Flight Tickets

  • Airline tickets are 100% non-refundable once issued
  • Some airlines may permit cancellations with heavy penalties
  • Name changes are not permitted on most discounted tickets
  • Refunds for cancellable tickets take 4-6 weeks to process

Transportation Services

  • 50% refund if cancelled 72+ hours before service
  • 25% refund if cancelled 24-72 hours before service
  • No refund for cancellations within 24 hours
  • No-shows will be charged 100% of the service cost

Special Circumstances

Force Majeure Events:

For cancellations due to natural disasters, political unrest, pandemics, or other unforeseen circumstances:

  • We will work with suppliers to obtain maximum possible refunds
  • Refunds will be processed as credits for future travel when possible
  • Processing may take 60-90 days during such events

Medical Emergencies:

With valid medical documentation:

  • 75% refund if cancelled 7+ days before travel
  • 50% refund if cancelled within 7 days of travel
  • Must provide doctor's certificate within 48 hours of cancellation

Refund Processing

  • Refunds will be processed within 15 working days of receiving cancellation request
  • Refunds will be issued via the original payment method
  • Bank processing times may add 3-5 additional business days
  • All refunds are subject to payment gateway processing fees (2-3%)

Note: This policy applies to all bookings made through TrawelPlay Hospitality. Some services may have stricter cancellation policies which will be communicated at the time of booking.

Our accounts team is available to clarify any questions regarding our refund policy.
