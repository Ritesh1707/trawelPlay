About Trawelplay Hospitality

Our Journey

Founded in 2015, Trawelplay Hospitality began with a simple vision: to become the most reliable B2B travel partner in India. What started as a small operation in Jaipur has grown into a pan-India destination management company serving hundreds of travel agents nationwide.

Over the years, we've built strong relationships with hotels, transport providers, and local guides across 50+ Indian destinations, allowing us to offer our partners the best rates and seamless service.
